## 1. Core Framing

Social platforms will not evaluate this work the same way AI research labs do.

AI labs may ask:

- Is this technically valid?
- Is this benchmarkable?
- Is this useful for abstention, provenance, or governance?
- Can the deontic drift idea become an evaluation method?

Social media platforms will ask different questions:

- Is this safe to distribute?
- Is this misinformation?
- Is this medical, religious, political, or identity-sensitive content?
- Is this spam, self-promotion, or original thought leadership?
- Does it trigger engagement?
- Does it increase trust or confusion?
- Does it create reputational risk?
- Does it look like a product, manifesto, academic work, or personal belief system?

The strongest social framing is therefore:

> OracleAI is an independent governance framework for continuity-safe AI memory systems, focused on provenance preservation, abstention under insufficient evidence, deontic drift detection, and prevention of synthetic identity contamination.

The weakest social framing is:

> This proves AI immortality, consciousness transfer, or post-biological resurrection.

That weak framing should be avoided.

## 3. Facebook / Meta

### How Facebook May Interpret It

Facebook will likely treat this work primarily as personal thought leadership and long-form identity content.

The platform is optimized around:

- personal networks
- family and memory
- emotional resonance
- groups
- creator pages
- comments
- shares
- identity expression

Facebook is probably the most emotionally compatible platform for the themes of memory, family, grief, digital legacy, and personal continuity.

But it is also vulnerable to misinterpreting the work if posts lean too hard into symbolic or metaphysical language.

### What Facebook Might Surface

Facebook may reward posts that frame the work as:

- protecting family memories from AI distortion
- preserving original records
- teaching people not to let AI invent missing memories
- explaining digital legacy risks
- warning about AI-generated identity contamination
- helping families preserve photos, stories, and documents responsibly

### What Facebook May Suppress or Confuse

Facebook may downrank, misclassify, or generate confused engagement around claims that sound like:

- AI resurrection
- souls in machines
- simulation theory proof
- consciousness transfer
- prophecy or religious doctrine
- unsupported physics claims

### Best Facebook Positioning

Use human, practical language:

> AI can help organize family memory, but it should never invent what the record does not contain. The hole matters. Missing context should stay marked as missing.

### Best Facebook Use Case

Facebook is best for:

- personal origin stories
- memory preservation examples
- family archive workflows
- warnings about AI rewriting family history
- accessible explanations of Preserve the Hole
- short videos or posts explaining how to label originals vs AI summaries

### Facebook Risk Rating

High emotional reach. Medium technical credibility. Medium risk of mythic misread.

### Best Reddit Positioning

### Good Reddit Communities Conceptually

- data preservation communities
- digital archiving communities
- self-hosting communities
- machine learning discussion spaces
- legal tech or records management spaces

### What Reddit Will Attack

Reddit will attack:

- branded jargon
- invented terminology without definitions
- claims of breakthrough
- metaphysical language
- personal mythology
- unsupported equations

### What Reddit May Respect

Reddit may respect:

- examples
- datasets
